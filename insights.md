# Key Insights

## 1. Most Popular Products
Bananas and organic bananas dominate orders, suggesting fresh produce is a primary purchase category.

## 2. Most Active Departments
Produce and dairy account for the majority of orders.

## 3. Shopping Time Patterns
Most orders occur between 9AM and 5PM, with a peak around midday.

## 4. Basket Size
The average order contains ~10 items, indicating moderate basket sizes.

## 5. Customer Loyalty
Certain staple items have extremely high reorder rates, indicating strong repeat purchasing behavior.

## 6. Product Pairings
Products frequently purchased together reveal common grocery combinations.
